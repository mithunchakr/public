Established in 1982

Family owned and operated since 1982, Sports Page Ski & Patio has been one of the top rated specialty ski and snowboard shops in the Northeast. Founded by Gary & Susan Higley, Sports Page has evolved from a multi-sport retail store into a one-stop ski & snowboard shop serving the needs of skiers & riders throughout the country.

We work hard to combine our years of industry knowledge, the latest in winter sports technology & friendly, personalized service to provide you with the best possible in-store & on-mountain experience.
